Things to Do

Ghent is a beautiful city with plenty to explore. Here are a few highlights worth visiting while you're here.

Hotel d'Hane Steenhuyse
Stunning 18th-century palace with rococo interiors
Belfry of Ghent
91-metre tower with panoramic views of the city
Gravensteen
Medieval castle of the Counts of Flanders
St Bavo's Cathedral
Home to the Ghent Altarpiece by the Van Eyck brothers
Graslei & Korenlei
Picturesque medieval waterfront along the Leie river
St Bavo's Abbey
Ruins of a medieval abbey
STAM
Ghent's city museum tracing the story of the city from medieval times to today

Ghent is also a wonderful city to simply wander around in, with charming streets and hidden corners waiting to be discovered. Try this city walk by Visit Gent

We would also love to suggest things to do based on your interests, just let us know if we can help!
